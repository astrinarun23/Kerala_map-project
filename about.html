About This Project

This website presents an interactive visualization of accident blackspots across various districts in Kerala.

Data Source: The data is based on a user-provided GeoJSON file containing detailed accident statistics for specific locations.

Purpose: The primary goal is to raise public awareness and provide accessible data for researchers, policymakers, and the general public to understand road safety challenges in the region.

Disclaimer: This is a demonstrational project. The data is used as provided and has not been independently verified.
